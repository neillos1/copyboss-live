Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! You get one free analyzer report to test it out — no credit card required.

Do my reports expire?

No, once purchased, you can access and download your reports anytime.

What happens after I buy a bundle?

You'll instantly unlock credits for the number of reports in your bundle.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel anytime from your dashboard. You'll retain access until the end of the billing period.

How long does it take to generate a report?

Reports are usually ready within 30–60 seconds, depending on video length.

What platforms are supported?

You can analyze TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and X (Twitter) videos.

Do you offer refunds?

Since reports are delivered instantly, refunds are generally not available. However, if you encounter a technical issue, contact support and we'll help.

Can I use CopyBoss on mobile?

Yes! CopyBoss works on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers — no app download needed.

How does billing work?

Subscriptions renew automatically every month unless you cancel. Bundles are one-time purchases and never expire until used.

Do you have a referral system?

Yes! Invite friends to CopyBoss and earn rewards when they sign up. You'll get bonuses added directly to your account.

What about the Community Hub?

All users can join the forum, but some advanced features, tips, and exclusive content are only available on the Pro plan.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures. Your videos are processed securely and deleted after analysis.

Can I analyze private videos?

Currently, we only support public videos from TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and X. Private video analysis is not available.
